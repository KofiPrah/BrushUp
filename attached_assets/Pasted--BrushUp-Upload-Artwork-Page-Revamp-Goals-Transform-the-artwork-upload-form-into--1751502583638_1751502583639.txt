# 🎨 BrushUp – Upload Artwork Page Revamp

## ✨ Goals

- Transform the artwork upload form into a **multi-step onboarding flow**
- Prioritize **clarity, visual delight**, and **progressive disclosure**
- Tailor experience for users with the **Artist** role
- Enable both quick submissions and detailed metadata
- Improve **critique engagement** by prompting users

---

## 🔁 Current Issues (Summary)

- Flat, form-like UI
- Optional fields lack guidance
- Critique request buried in inputs
- Functional, but not welcoming or engaging

---

## ✅ Proposed UX Flow (Multi-Step Wizard)

### **Step 1: Upload Artwork**
- Drag-and-drop or browse
- Show full-bleed preview
- Required: Image

### **Step 2: Title & Description**
- Required: Title
- Optional but encouraged: Description
- Prompts:
  - _“What’s this piece called?”_
  - _“Tell us the story behind it”_

### **Step 3: Medium & Dimensions**
- Medium dropdown + custom entry
- Dimensions placeholder: e.g., _“24x36 inches”_

### **Step 4: Tags & Folder**
- Tags: comma-separated or chip input
- Folder: dropdown or thumbnail cards

### **Step 5: Seeking Critique**
- Toggle: “Yes, I’d like feedback”
- Optional: Checkboxes for focus areas (Technique, Composition, etc.)
- Short explainer of how critiques work

### **Final Step: Review & Upload**
- Show summary card of input
- Upload with visual progress and playful copy:  
  _“Uploading your brilliance…”_

---

## 🛠 Backend Readiness

✅ `/api/artworks/` endpoint supports:

- `title`, `description`, `image`, `medium`, `dimensions`, `tags`, `folder`, `seeking_critique`

No backend changes needed.

---

## ⚛ Frontend Plan (React)

Refactor `ArtworkUpload.jsx`:

- Use `useState(step)` to control wizard flow
- Break into subcomponents:
  - `Step1UploadImage`
  - `Step2Details`
  - `Step3Meta`
  - `Step4TagsFolder`
  - `Step5Critique`
  - `Step6Review`
- Final submission: assemble `FormData`, POST to `/api/artworks/`

Use:
- Bootstrap 5 cards
- Bootstrap Icons (brush, upload, layers, etc.)
- Transitions (fade/slide)
